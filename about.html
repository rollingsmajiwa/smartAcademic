About Us

Modernizing School Management and Exam Supervision

SmartAcademic is a secure website built to simplify daily school operations for both teachers and learners. Our platform focuses on two key administrative challenges: manual attendance tracking and examination management. By replacing a traditional paper logbooks, the system introduces automated clock-in and clock-out tracking that operates strickly within the school vicinity. It also provides a secure environment for digital assessment where exam duration are strickly enforced by automated countdown timers, ensuring fairness for all learners.

Secure Exam Engine

Automated countdown timers force immediate submission when time elapses.

Geo-Fenced Attendance

Verifies logs instantly when the teacher is within school boundaries.

Centralised Student Portal

Immediate access to assessment and progress reports.

Why Choose Us

No More Paper Logs: Teachers sign in and out digitally on their devices, saving time and reducing paperwork.
Location Security: Attendance can only be verified when the teacher is physically within the school.
Guaranteed Exam Fairness: When a student takes an exam, the screen locks down and automatically submits their work the exact second time runs out.
